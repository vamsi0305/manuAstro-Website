            FAQ's

            How can astrology actually help me in real life?

            Astrology, when applied logically, reveals thetiming and natureof opportunities and challenges. It helps you make informed decisions about career, relationships, finance, and health saving time, energy, and effort.

            Are consultations done online or in person?

            You can choose either. Manu Ji offers online video/phone consultations for clients worldwide, as well as in-person sessions (subject to availability and location).

            How do I book a consultation?

            Simply visit the Book Consultation section, choose your service type, select your preferred time, and complete payment securely through our portal. You’ll receive confirmation and meeting details instantly.

            What information do I need to provide?

            YourDate of Birth, Time of Birth,andPlace of Birthare essential for accurate astrological analysis. For Vaastu consultations, property layout or floor plan helps too.

            Are my details and discussions confidential?

            Absolutely. Every consultation is 100 % private and protected by strict confidentiality. We never share client data.

            How soon can I expect results after following remedies?

            It varies per individual. Remedies are designed forlogical alignment, not miracles. Many clients begin noticing clarity and positive changes within weeks.

            Do you guarantee specific outcomes?

            No. We provide direction and solutions based on astrological patterns, but results depend on effort, timing, and karma. Our focus isguidance with realism, not superstition.

            Can I reschedule or cancel a consultation?

            Yes, rescheduling is possible if informed 24 hours in advance. (Exact policy details can be updated later.)
